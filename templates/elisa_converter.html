ELISA Document Converter Tutorial

Let's learn how to convert ELISA documentation

What This Tool Does

The ELISA Document Converter is a specialized tool that:

  • Extracts content from Boster ELISA kit documentation ("outside" document)
  • Reorganizes content into an Innovative Research template ("inside" document)
  • Replaces all instances of "Boster" with "Innovative Research"
  • Removes all instances of "PicoKine"
  • Applies proper formatting and styling to the document
  • Inserts catalog and lot numbers in the correct locations

This tutorial will show you how to use this specialized converter.

Uploading the Outside Document

First, upload the Boster ELISA kit documentation:

  1. Drag and drop the Boster ELISA document into the first upload area, or
  2. Click the "Browse Files" button to select the file

This is the source document containing all the content that needs to be extracted.

Example filename: EK1586_Mouse_KLK1Kallikrein_1_ELISA_Kit_PicoKine_Datasheet.docx

Uploading the Template Document

Next, upload the Innovative Research template document:

  1. Drag and drop the template document into the second upload area, or
  2. Click the "Browse Files" button to select the file

This is the template that will be populated with content from the outside document.

Example filename: Boster Template.docx

Entering Catalog and Lot Numbers

Provide the catalog and lot numbers for the document:

  1. Enter the catalog number in the "Catalog Number" field
  2. Enter the lot number in the "Lot Number" field

These numbers will be inserted in the appropriate places in the final document.

Both fields are required to process the document.

Converting and Downloading

After uploading both documents and entering the catalog/lot numbers:

  1. Click the "Convert Document" button
  2. Wait while the document is processed (this may take a minute)
  3. Once complete, you'll see a download link for your converted document
  4. Click "Download Converted Document" to save the result to your computer

The converter will automatically extract all required sections, apply formatting, and make the necessary text replacements.

Need help? Click the tutorial button at any time to see this guide again.
